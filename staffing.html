Why Create a Candidate Pool of International Nurses?

The United States and Canada are facing a critical nursing shortage—the turnover rate for nurses can reach up to 37%. Demand is high, but the number of nurses is low, especially in rural areas. It’s just as difficult for staffing agencies to fill nursing positions.

There’s a simple solution: international nurses. Until now, connecting qualified international nurses with jobs in the U.S. and Canada was a complicated, lengthy process, prohibiting staffing agencies from finding the candidates they needed. We’re changing that.

JobGraze helps staffing agencies find international nursing candidates to better support their clients, fill more positions, and provide seamless patient care.
