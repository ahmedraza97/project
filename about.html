Our Story

Every day in the U.S., 10,000 people turn 65 years old, and the number of seniors in Canada is growing six times faster than children. But more nurses and physicians are suffering from burnout. The gap in healthcare is dramatically increasing.

In 2021, one of our co-founders, Alexis, was a healthcare provider and experienced a turnover of 100 caregivers within 90 days. With the high costs of contract help and inefficient solutions, this took a toll on nurses and patients.

To fix this, Alexis partnered with co-founder Jen to build a healthcare staffing solution for experienced nurses looking for new opportunities in the US and Canada. JobGraze is the only AI-powered platform to connect international nurses with employers in need of full-time staff. 

We’re combating the nationwide nursing shortage by simplifying the process of hiring international nurses, and ensuring the highest quality of care for patients.
