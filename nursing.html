Most, if not all, employers require a resume for international nursing job applications. To make it effective:
-Talk about your hands-on experience and what you've learned.
-Share your education, certifications, and any specialized training.
-Highlight both your technical skills and people skills, especially your digital know-how.
-Keep it specific and personal—avoid sounding too generic.
