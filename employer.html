Why Hire International Nurses?

There are approximately 1.2 million nurse job vacancies nationwide, and the exodus of nurses continues. Understaffed hospitals and practices face higher costs and struggle to meet patient needs.

We work with international nurses who are highly qualified and are eager to move to the U.S. or Canada for better career opportunities. Hiring international nurses quickly fills gaps in your workforce, dramatically cuts costs, and improves patient care.
